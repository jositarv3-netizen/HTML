Lightning heat

Lightning can reach temperatures of up to 30,000 degrees Celsius, which is about five times hotter than the surface of the sun.

impacts of lightning

Lightning can cause wildfires, power outages, and even damage buildings. It is important to take precautions during thunderstorms to stay safe.
