Prepare Maggie

  1. Get a bowl and fill it with water.
  2. Place Maggie noodles in the bowl.
  3. Microwave the bowl for 3 minutes.
  4. Carefully remove the bowl from the microwave and stir the noodles.
  5. Let it cool for a minute before eating. Enjoy your meal!
